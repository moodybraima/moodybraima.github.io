Brief Descriptions of Projects

PacMen Factory

PacMan is a game you can play by adding as many pacmen as you wish then move the images to bounce against the confines of the rectangular area on the screen.

Eyes Movement

In this app, a pair of eyes can track the movements of the mouse. The codes allow the eyes to track the coordinates of the mouse in the screen of the computer.

Bush Tracking

This application allows the user to programmatically track the travel of the bus between the MIT campus and Harvard University.
